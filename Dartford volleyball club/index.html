Dartford Volleyball Club

Our Training sessions

Wednesday evenings from 8pm – 10pm Men’s and Women’s National League training session. Sessions take place over two courts and are aimed primarily at the National League teams, but those who want to train at a higher level may also attend. Thursday evenings from 8.30pm – 10.00pm Open session for all abilities. Sessions take place on two courts and have coaches available. These sessions are open to all: men’s, women’s, juniors, beginner and nation league. All sessions take place at Dartford Technology College, Heath Lane, DARTFORD, Kent, DA1 2LY
